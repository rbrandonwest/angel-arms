Your impact

What your gift makes possible.

$25

Provides a week of groceries for a family through our food pantry partnership.

$50

Funds an hour of one-on-one counseling for an individual in recovery.

$100

Covers a week of essentials (formula, diapers, supplies) for an Angel Arms infant.

$250

Sponsors a family education workshop on caring for a child with NAS.

$500

Helps with one month of utilities or rent for a family in emergency transition.

$1,000+

Underwrites a multi-week support program — counseling, case work, and community.
