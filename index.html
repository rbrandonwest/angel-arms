Our priority · Brighter Path

For the smallest among us — and the families who love them.

The Brighter Path program is the heart of Angel Arms. We empower infants and children affected by Neonatal Abstinence Syndrome and other drug exposure with a holistic approach: educational, nutritional, social-emotional, counseling, and developmental support — all under one nurturing roof.

How we help

Care that meets you exactly where you are.

Counseling & Casework

One-on-one and family counseling guided by trained professionals — judgment-free and rooted in dignity.

Addiction Recovery

Long-term support for individuals in recovery, integrated with sound community practice and mentorship.

Infant & Family Support

Through Angel Arms, holistic care for NAS infants and a circle of support for birth parents and caregivers.

Housing Assistance

Helping families find stable shelter and the resources to keep it — connected to local partners.

Employment Support

Coaching, resume preparation, and partnerships that open the door to meaningful, sustainable work.

Prison Release Support

Re-entry guidance, accountability, and connection to community — because second chances begin with belonging.

Food Assistance

In partnership with the county food bank, ensuring families don't have to choose between basic needs.

Called to Care

National and international service teams mobilized to meet urgent needs with cultural sensitivity.
