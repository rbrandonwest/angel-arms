Our services

Care for infants, children, and the families who love them.

Angel Arms Brighter Path is the heart of our work. The broader Faith Forward umbrella also offers counseling, recovery support, and family services — all free of charge.

Angel Arms · Brighter Path

A safe haven for infants exposed to substances — and for the families learning how to love them well.

Angel Arms provides a short-term safe atmosphere for infants and children suffering from substance abuse exposure. We deliver managed care for NAS infants, while serving birth parents, caregivers, and entire family systems with education, awareness, and support.

NAS Infant Managed Care

Specialized, professionally supervised short-term care for infants experiencing Neonatal Abstinence Syndrome.

ITCM Holistic Approach

Integrated Trauma-Care Methodology addressing each child's educational, nutritional, social-emotional, counseling, and developmental needs.

Birth Parent & Caregiver Support

Counseling, recovery resources, and parenting support for the adults in each child's life — because the child's future is bound to theirs.

Family Education & Awareness

Workshops and one-on-one teaching for caregivers, foster families, and communities about NAS, trauma, and infant development.

Individualized Care Plans

Every child receives their own targeted plan — skills, activities, and milestones tailored to their development and history.

Group Therapy & Mentorship

Peer connection for parents in recovery, mentorship for older children in care, and group support that fights isolation.

Faith Forward Ministries

Walking with adults and families.

Counseling

Individual, couples, and family counseling delivered by trained professionals. Faith-informed when desired, never imposed.

Addiction Recovery Support

Long-term recovery support that integrates sound community practice, mentorship, and pathways to treatment.

Housing Assistance

Connecting individuals and families to stable shelter, transitional housing, and the resources to keep it.

Employment Assistance

Resume preparation, interview coaching, and warm introductions to employer partners across the region.

Life Coaching

Goal-setting, accountability, and step-by-step planning — because moving forward is easier with someone walking alongside you.

Prison Release Support

Re-entry planning, accountability circles, and community connection — because returning home should mean truly belonging.

Family Relationship Counseling

Healing fractured relationships between parents, children, spouses, and siblings — restoring trust at the pace of those involved.

Food & Emergency Assistance

In partnership with the county food bank — connecting families to groceries, diapers, hygiene supplies, and emergency support.

Called to Care

National and international service teams mobilized to meet urgent humanitarian needs with cultural sensitivity and integrity.

Special Interest Groups

Peer-led groups for unplanned pregnancy support, grief, parenting, men's and women's communities, and more.

Community Outreach

Festivals, holiday meals, school supply drives, and neighborhood events — building the kind of community where no one is forgotten.

Casework & Advocacy

Help navigating benefits, court dates, paperwork, and the systems that can feel impossible to face alone.
